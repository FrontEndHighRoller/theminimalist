Roy’s Peak, Wanaka, New Zealand

Published on July 8, 2025 · 5 min read

Roy’s Peak in New Zealand is known for its breathtaking alpine views, sweeping over Lake Wanaka and the Southern Alps. This hike has become iconic among travelers seeking natural beauty and rewarding adventures.

"It’s never too late and you’re never too old to start over. Every day is a chance to make new decisions, try new things, and live your story."

How to Get There

The trailhead starts just outside of Wanaka. It’s a moderately difficult hike that takes about 5–6 hours round-trip. Make sure to start early to catch the sunrise!

What to Bring

  • Water and snacks
  • Layers — it can get windy at the top
  • A camera (trust us, you’ll want it)
  • Hiking boots with good grip

Whether you’re traveling solo or with friends, Roy’s Peak is one of those hikes that stays with you. The climb may challenge you, but the view at the summit makes every step worth it.
