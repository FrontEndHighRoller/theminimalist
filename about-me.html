About Me

I’m a passionate solo traveler from Czechia who’s wandered across 29 countries and counting. Over the years, I’ve embraced minimalist travel and long-term backpacking, chasing adventure and self-discovery on every continent I visit.

Southern Africa – 4 Months of Wild Beauty

My most recent journey led me deep into the heart of Southern and Eastern Africa: South Africa, Namibia, Tanzania, Zanzibar, and Kenya. For four unforgettable months, I backpacked through deserts, safaris, coastal villages, and bustling cities — immersing myself in vibrant cultures, wildlife, and natural wonders.

New Zealand – Two Years in Nature’s Playground

Before Africa, I called New Zealand home for nearly two years. From the fjords of the South Island to the volcanic beauty of the North, this peaceful country shaped my connection to nature and outdoor living.

Caribbean & Americas – Island Rhythms and Big City Dreams

I also ventured through the Caribbean with visits to Jamaica, the Dominican Republic, and Colombia, soaking up island life, Afro-Caribbean culture, and sunshine. Then I explored city life in the U.S., including unforgettable trips to Miami and New York.

Why I Travel

Travel has taught me resilience, humility, and curiosity. Whether I’m navigating a night bus through Kenya or sharing stories over dinner in a Colombian village, I’ve found that the world opens up when we step into the unknown.

Through this blog, I aim to inspire others to explore with an open heart — and maybe pack a little lighter.
